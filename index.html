Setting the Scene

Sketchbook one

Setting the scene is an Artist Residency in Coundon and Witton Park. Organised by Northern Heartlands. As part of this residency, artists Ronan Devlin and Dominic Smith will set up space in your community centres where you can come along and help form a creative community.

It will be practical and involve creating audio content, recording the sounds in your environment, hosting interviews, recording musicians, and working towards making a radio broadcast. It will also involve making zines (small self-made books), posters, stickers and badges to create a scene that celebrates local culture.
